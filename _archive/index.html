Who I am

After obtaining a Bachelors degree in Exercise Movement Science from Missouri State University I moved to Kansas City in pursuit of an undiscovered passion. After obtaining a job as a Technical Recruiter, and learning about software development, it became increasingly obvious what that passion was. At a certain point the urge to follow that calling became too difficult to resist and it was time to move beyond self-study and Youtube videos. Seeing the success of Centriq graduates and knowing close friends who had attended their Full Stack Development program, it was clear that Centriq was the best choice for me. When I'm not coding I can be found in the kitchen trying new recipes, in the gym, watching tv or playing video games with my friends.

Mission Statement

One of the most alluring aspects of software development for me is the endless sea of knowledge. Between the new technology that, almost seems to get created on a daily basis and the endless ways to apply that technology I have truly only begun to dip my toes in. Although my training with Centriq will come to an end in December, 2020, my desire to learn is ceaseless. I hope to find an employer who will give me the hands on experience and exposure needed to grow my knowledge and who will let me apply my skills to make a positive impact on their team and company as a whole.
